Authenticator Pro generates Two Factor Authentication (2FA) codes for your online accounts. TOTP, HOTP, mOTP (Mobile-OTP) and Steam are supported. The generated codes are one time tokens that provide an extra layer of security to your online accounts. After scanning a simple QR code, your account is protected.

<b>Compatibility</b>
Authenticator Pro is compatible with most providers and accounts.

<b>Backup / Restore</b>
Backup your authenticators with strong encryption. En caso de que pierda o cambie de teléfono, siempre podrá acceder a sus cuentas. Guardar en la nube o en su dispositivo.

<b>Importar</b>
Migre fácilmente sus cuentas a Authenticator Pro desde aplicaciones alternativas compatibles.

<b>Modo oscuro</b>
Authenticator Pro tiene un hermoso diseño Android Material inspirado en temas claros u oscuros.

<b>Iconos</b>
Encuentre fácilmente sus autenticadores con logotipos de marca e iconos reconocidos junto a cada código.

<b>Categorías:</b>
Organice sus autenticadores en categorías.

<b>Desconectado con pocos permisos</b>
Authenticator Pro solo requiere un permiso único y no requiere acceso a Internet para funcionar

<b>Personalización</b>
Establecer iconos y renombrar. You can also arrange your authenticators in any order you like so you can find them easily.

<b>Wear OS</b>
Authenticator Pro is also available as a Wear OS companion app. Quickly view your authenticators directly from your watch.

<b>Seguridad</b>
Set a password and use biometric authentification to protect your codes.

2 Factor authentication provides an extra layer of security to your accounts by requiring the use of a one time code to log in. In addition to your username and password, you will need a code provided by Authenticator Pro. So even if your login details are compromised, your account remains safe.
